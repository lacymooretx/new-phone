Coming Soon

Upcoming features and improvements planned for end users. These are actively in development and will be documented here once released.

What Is Coming Next

The Aspendora Connect is under active development. The features listed below are planned for upcoming releases. Each feature will get its own documentation page in this user guide when it becomes available.

Features on this page are in various stages of development. Your administrator can provide more specific timing for features that are important to your organization.

Mobile App Coming Soon

Native mobile applications for iOS and Android that let you make and receive calls on your extension from your phone. The iOS app uses Apple CallKit for native call integration, and the Android app uses ConnectionService for seamless call handling. Your extension rings on your mobile device just like it does on your desk phone.

Desktop App Coming Soon

A standalone desktop application for Windows and macOS that runs outside your browser. It includes a system tray icon for quick access, incoming call notifications even when the app is minimized, and all the same features as the web softphone in a dedicated window.

SMS Messaging Coming Soon

Send and receive text messages (SMS and MMS) directly from the Aspendora Connect application. Conversations are threaded by contact, so you can see your full message history in one place. Messages can be routed to queues for shared team inboxes, making it easy to handle customer texts as a group.

Video Calling Coming Soon

Start video calls with coworkers directly from the application. Video calling works through the same web softphone interface, with the option to turn your camera on or off during a call. No separate video conferencing software needed for quick internal face-to-face conversations.

Presence & Status Coming Soon

Show your availability to coworkers in real time. Set your status to Available, Busy, Away, or Do Not Disturb, and see the status of others in the directory. Presence updates automatically based on your call activity, so coworkers know when you are on a call without having to check.

Contact Directory Coming Soon

A searchable directory of all users in your organization with their extension numbers, email addresses, and departments. Click any contact to call them instantly. The directory also includes external contacts that your administrator has added, giving you a single place to find anyone you need to reach.

Click-to-Call Browser Extension Coming Soon

A browser extension for Chrome and Edge that detects phone numbers on any web page and turns them into clickable links. Click a phone number on any website and it automatically dials through your Aspendora Connect softphone. Great for calling numbers from CRM systems, websites, and email without manually copying and pasting.

Voicemail Transcription Coming Soon

AI-powered automatic transcription of your voicemail messages. Instead of listening to each message, read a text transcript right in your voicemail inbox. Transcriptions are also included in voicemail-to-email notifications so you can quickly scan messages without playing audio.

Transfer to Voicemail Coming Soon

Send an active call directly to another user's voicemail box without ringing their phone. Useful when you know a coworker is unavailable and want to let the caller leave a message immediately rather than waiting through multiple rings.

Headset Integration Coming Soon

Native support for headset controls from popular brands like Jabra, Poly, and Plantronics. Answer and hang up calls using the button on your headset, and mute or adjust volume without switching to the browser. Headset status indicators (LED busy light) will also reflect your call state.

Dark Mode Improvements Coming Soon

Enhanced dark mode with improved contrast, better color choices for accessibility, and automatic switching based on your operating system preferences. The current theme toggle will be expanded to support system-auto, light, and dark modes with smoother transitions between themes.

Have a Feature Request?

If there is a feature you would like to see in the Aspendora Connect, let your administrator know. They collect feedback from users and work with the development team to prioritize new features. Your input helps shape the direction of the platform.

Stay Updated
When features from this page are released, they will be moved to their own documentation pages with full usage instructions. Check back here periodically to see what is new, or ask your administrator about upcoming releases.
